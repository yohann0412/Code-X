Web Development and Design

Website Development Challenge

Participants will be given a prompt and they will be asked to design a website addressing that specific challenge. The various elements of a well functioning website will be judged, which include the layout, design, user interface, usability etc.
No of prompts : 1 prompt per division
Divisions: 9th and 10th grade - Junior, 11th and 12th grade - Senior.
Time allotted - 1 week.
Team member capacity - Maximum of 3 members per team.

Problem Solving

Problem solving Programming Competition

Participants of each grade will be given 5 questions (challenges) in increasing order of difficulty.Not only programming skills, but also problem-solving skills are required to succeed in this challenge. The participants will be asked to answer each question within the time allotted and they must submit their codes by the deadline else it will result in disqualification.
No of prompts : 5 prompts per grade (time per question- 40 minutes)
Difficulty of questions: Intermediate
No. of divisions : 4 (9th, 10th, 11th 12th)
Time allotted : 4 hours (one day challenge)
Submission mode : submission on Google forms in the form of a Microsoft Word document.
Team member capacity: No teams - Individual competition.
Languages accepted - Python, C++, java.

Judge 1: Aryaman
Judge 2: Shubham
