About Us

Our Mission

“First, solve the problem. Then, write the code.”

- John Johnson

With the movement of the world towards a more technologically advanced future, the growth of coding as an essential skill is inevitable. When we used to talk about languages, it referred to the languages spoken around the world by people such as French, Spanish, English etc. Now, this definition is being challenged with the emergence of coding languages such as Java, Python, C++ etc. As many people say, coding is the language of the future as we become more interconnected with technology.
CodeX is an Inter-school coding competition for grades 9 to 12. It consists of two categories- Website development and programming. The eligible programming languages for the competition are Python, Java, C++ and Qalb(For Arabic programming).
With so many coding/programming competitions out there, what makes us unique?
Along with all the aspects of a coding competition, we inculcate additional features which are usually not found in other competitions. The integration of Qalb, a programming language in Arabic, will allow Emirati schools to participate in the competition along with programmers in other mainstream languages. The addition of a website development category also brings a variety to this competition as website development is also gaining popularity with the increase in website creation. Along with the competition, there is also the inclusion of interactive programming workshops conducted by professionals.
The aim of this competition is to ignite the passion for computer science among students by allowing them to explore the wonderful art of competitive programming and to develop their problem-solving skills which will come in handy outside the coding world.
We hope to see you there!
